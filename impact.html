Impact

Transforming eczema management from reactive treatment to proactive prevention

Why SkinGuard?

Eczema, skin inflammation, and chronic dry skin are common skin issues nowadays, affecting millions of teenagers and adults across the United States, imposing significant physical and emotional burdens on daily life.

According to clinical research and public health data, approximately 10% of U.S. children and adolescents (ages 12–17) live with eczema, while the condition persists or develops in 7–10% of adults—translating to over 30 million Americans grappling with these skin challenges.

The Impact

Impact on Teenagers

For teenagers, these skin problems go far beyond physical discomfort; they also have emotional consequences. Eczema flare-ups often appear on visible areas like the face, arms, and legs, leading to a lack of self-confidence and social anxiety.

Impact on Adults

Adults face different challenges: chronic skin irritation can impact professional relationships, as noticeable inflammation can affect confidence in the workplace. Recurring flare-ups also add to their workload outside of work.

Emotional Toll

Studies show that individuals with chronic skin conditions are more likely to experience anxiety and depression, driven by the frustration of unpredictable symptoms and the stigma of "visible" health issues.

Beyond Individual Hardship

These skin issues also place a hidden burden on families: parents of affected teens often juggle doctor appointments, treatment routines, and emotional support, while adults may face missed workdays or reduced productivity due to discomfort.

In a nation that values health and quality of life, the widespread impact of these skin conditions underscores the urgent need for accessible, proactive solutions. For millions of Americans, managing eczema, inflammation, and dry skin isn't just a matter of skincare—it's a daily fight to reclaim comfort, confidence, and control over their lives.

Our Solution

SkinGuard: Smart Prevention for Eczema

SkinGuard is a smart and convenient device that connects via a mobile app, integrating datasets and artificial intelligence technology, specifically designed for eczema sufferers.

It shifts the focus from passive treatment to proactive prevention, helping children, teenagers, and their families manage this chronic condition. Unlike creams/medications that only work after an eczema flare-up, SkinGuard focuses on prevention.

It combines personal medical history, environmental factors, and big data analytics to provide clear, personalized solutions, transforming prevention into a digital, healthier lifestyle habit.
